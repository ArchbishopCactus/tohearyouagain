**BECAUSE OF YOU**  
Is it wrong of me to get upset when I see you?
To fill with rage when you talk to another?
To feel pain when you smile?  

Is it wrong of me to clench my jaw when you enter my sight?
To shake my leg when you fiddle with your hair?
To lose my breath when you look in my direction?  

Is it wrong of me to cry when you're away?
And to scream when you're not around?  

Is it wrong of me to break my nails
And to cut my skin
And to hit a wall
And to throw things
And to pull my hair
And to slit my wrists
And to walk into traffic
And to burn my hands
And to run endlessly
And to starve myself
And to live in the night
And to choke myself
And to hurt so much inside  

Because of you?
